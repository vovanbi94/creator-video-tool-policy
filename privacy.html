Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01

Tiem cua boi boi is a creator-focused video workflow application that helps users prepare short-form video content, manage captions, and publish finalized content to their authorized social media accounts.

Information We Collect

The service may collect basic account information provided through the user's authorization process, such as account identifier, display name, and profile information made available by the connected platform.

The service may also process video files, captions, and related metadata that users choose to provide for content preparation and publishing.

How We Use Information

We use the information only to provide the requested video workflow features, including preparing content, managing captions, connecting user accounts, and publishing content after user authorization.

Access Tokens

When users connect an account, the service may receive an access token from the connected platform. Access tokens are used only to perform actions authorized by the user, such as publishing content to the user's connected account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share user data with third parties except as required to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or interact with platforms that the user has chosen to connect.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain security, troubleshoot issues, or comply with applicable legal requirements.

User Control

Users may disconnect their connected accounts or revoke authorization through the connected platform's account or app settings.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information and access tokens. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
